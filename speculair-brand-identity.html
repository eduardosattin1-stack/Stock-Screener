01 — Brand

The posture behind the product.

Most screeners sell tools and hope you find the edge. SpeculAIr sells the edge — a tested investment system with the receipts attached. The brand should feel as honest, precise, and quietly confident as the product itself.

The Name

Speculate + AI + Air. To speculate is to look forward; AI is the engine; air is the clarity we offer in a market drowning in noise. Read it "spec-u-LAIR" — the AI lives inside the word, never shouting.

The Promise

Here are today's picks from a strategy with a published track record. Here's the reason each one made the cut, in plain language. Here's how the algorithm is doing live this month. No black box. No theatre.

The Posture

Confident, not loud. Technical, not jargon-filled. Editorial, not corporate. We talk to a private investor the way a senior analyst would — precise, opinionated, willing to flag uncertainty.

05 — Voice & Tone

Precise, opinionated, and willing to flag doubt.

We don't oversell. We don't hide behind disclaimers. We give the user a clear read and tell them when our own confidence is low. Think senior analyst, not influencer; think Mercury, not Robinhood.

We say things like

DEC scored 0.93 — strong quality and deep value, but momentum is weak. A patient setup, not a chase.
The BORING basket has returned +27% over the last 18 months. Here's the full track record, including the drawdowns.
Only 6 of 13 factors evaluated for this name. Composite capped at 0.75 until coverage improves.
The model says BUY. Here's what the model has been wrong about lately.

We don't say things like

Unlock the secret AI signals Wall Street doesn't want you to see.
Hot pick of the day. Don't miss it.
Powered by next-generation deep learning quantum algorithms.
This is not financial advice. We are not liable for anything. Consult a professional. (Buried in 8pt grey footer.)

07 — Rules

A short list. The system is the rule.

Brand systems collapse under exception. These rules are non-negotiable. Everything else is interpretation — and interpretation should always defer to restraint.

Do

+
Always lowercase the wordmark — "speculAIr," never "SPECULAIR" except in spaced caps for navigation labels.
+
Render the "AI" in Lavender every time. It is the signature element of the brand.
+
Use Signal Green sparingly — for the logomark, primary actions, and positive signals. Restraint amplifies it.
+
Default to Deep Sea backgrounds. Light surfaces are an exception, never the rule.
+
Pair JetBrains Mono with Fraunces italic for editorial moments. The contrast is the voice.

Don't

×
Don't gradient the logomark, add drop shadows, or rotate it. The mark is geometric and flat.
×
Don't recolor the "AI." If lavender doesn't work on the surface, recolor the surface.
×
Don't pair the brand with stock photography of traders, charts, or skyscrapers. Use UI screenshots instead.
×
Don't use Signal Green as a large background fill. It belongs in marks, accents, and data — not posters.
×
Don't introduce additional fonts. Three is the system. Adding a fourth dilutes the voice.

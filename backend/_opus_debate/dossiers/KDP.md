# KDP — Keurig Dr Pepper — Interrogator Dossier (Event-Driven Special Situation)

**Frame:** This is NOT a quality/compounding thesis. KDP is judged as a SUM-OF-PARTS / spin + index-flow special situation: the spread between the live price (~$30.85, Jun 22 2026) and a separation-resolved value, gated on the tax-free spin of "Global Coffee Co." from "Beverage Co.", with a downside floor set by the intrinsic/recovery value of the combined enterprise if the spin slips or breaks.

## 1. THE EVENT — IS IT REAL, DATED, AND BINDING?

**Real: YES.** On its 2025 strategy reset, KDP committed to separate into two independent, U.S.-listed pure-plays — **Beverage Co.** (North American refreshment: Dr Pepper, Snapple, Canada Dry, Mott's, energy/sports) and **Global Coffee Co.** (Keurig single-serve + the newly acquired **JDE Peet's** global coffee platform). The structure is a **tax-free spin of Global Coffee Co.** — the cleanest, most index-flow-relevant separation form (two S&P-listable entities, mechanical passive re-weighting on effective date).

**Acquisition leg: FIRED.** The enabling transaction — KDP's **~$18B acquisition of JDE Peet's** — **closed April 1, 2026** (KDP acquired essentially all shares). Funded with ~$9B long-term debt, ~$8.5B equity capital (incl. a $4.5B Beverage Co. convertible preferred from private equity, ~$7B PE raise), and assumption of ~$5B JDE Peet's bonds. So the hard, binary regulatory/financing leg is DONE. What remains is the spin mechanics, not a deal-close risk.

**Dated: NO — this is the load-bearing weakness.** The spin is **UNDATED**. Management language as of the Q1 2026 call (Apr 23, 2026): operational readiness targeted **end-2026**, with official separation **"likely early 2027,"** and — critically — **timing explicitly conditioned on "achievement of key milestones, including appropriate leverage levels at each company, AND supportive market conditions."** That is a soft, contingent, management-controlled trigger, not a fixed record/effective date. There is no PDUFA-style hard date, no proxy/vote window with a fixed clock, no Form 10 effectiveness date in hand.

**Binding: PARTIALLY.** The board has committed and the acquisition is sunk cost, which raises the bar against abandonment. But "supportive market conditions" is a genuine out-clause: the spin can slip quarters if equity/credit markets are unfriendly or if deleveraging lags.

## 2. CREDIBILITY OF MANAGEMENT ON HITTING IT

- **Cofer/Oliveira split leadership already named** (Tim Cofer → Beverage Co. CEO; Rafael Oliveira → Global Coffee Co. CEO). Naming both CEOs pre-separation is a credible commitment signal — they are building the org charts, not floating a concept.
- **Financing executed cleanly** (PE preferred + bond raise + JDE close on schedule) — management delivered the hard part on time, which raises confidence on the soft part.
- **Q1 2026 guidance reaffirmed** low-double-digit EPS growth; EPS $1.35 (+22.7% reported), revenue TTM $16.94B (+9.2%). The operating business is funding the deleveraging, not bleeding.
- **Counter:** every prior communication has pushed the realization rightward (year-end 2026 → "operational readiness" end-2026 → "likely early 2027"). The trajectory of the DATE is slipping even as the PLAN strengthens.

## 3. THE VERIFIED DOWNSIDE FLOOR

The floor here is NOT a deal-break price (the deal closed) — it is the **standalone/recovery value of the combined enterprise if the spin is delayed or never separates the SoP discount**. KDP at ~$30.85 trades at forward P/E ~12.9x on a consumer-staples cash machine with a 3.0% dividend (well-covered) and reaffirmed double-digit EPS growth. A staples beverage business at 13-14x forward is a credible support: a no-spin "stuck conglomerate" still earns and pays. The dossier downside floor of **$28.50** (~7.6% below live) is defensible as the bear/no-catalyst anchor — a low-double-digit multiple staple with a safe yield does not collapse; the dividend + buyback authorization + the optionality of non-core asset monetization (management flagged it as a deleveraging avenue) backstop the price. The floor is REAL but THIN relative to the upside (only ~$2.35 of downside cushion).

## 4. RED FLAGS

- 🔴 **Catalyst is UNDATED and serially right-shifting** ("likely early 2027," market-conditions out-clause) — the single biggest disqualifier from AGGRESSIVE-ENTRY treatment. This is SOFT, not PENDING_HARD.
- 🔴 **Elevated leverage (~4.5x combined net)** post-JDE — the spin is GATED on getting leverage down at BOTH entities. Deleveraging risk is the gating-item, and it's interest-rate/FCF dependent.
- 🔴 **Coffee drag** — JDE Peet's integration + soft global coffee category is a near-term EPS/multiple headwind ("the coffee drag is temporary" is the bull's own framing, i.e. it is currently a drag).
- 🔴 **Modest spread.** SoP R:R 1.87 (target $37.70 / floor $28.50 vs $31.71 reference; live now ~$30.85, so spread slightly wider than dossier). Upside-to-target ~22%, downside-to-floor ~7.6%. Asymmetric, but the absolute spread is small for a no-hard-date wait that could run 9-12+ months.

## 5. GREEN FLAGS

- 🟢 **Hard enabling leg already fired** (JDE close Apr 1 2026) — removes the binary deal-break risk that kills most arb setups.
- 🟢 **Tax-free spin = clean index-flow SoP** — when effective, mechanical passive re-weighting + pure-play re-rating (coffee comps vs beverage comps both above the blended conglomerate multiple) is a genuine value-unlock.
- 🟢 **Both CEOs named, financing closed, EPS guidance reaffirmed** — execution credibility is high; this is not a vaporware spin.
- 🟢 **Defended floor** — a profitable staple with a covered 3% yield and buyback support bounds the loss.

## 6. EVENT VERDICT

A genuine, well-sponsored sum-of-parts spin whose **enabling transaction has already fired**, but whose **value-unlock event is UNDATED, contingent on deleveraging + "supportive market conditions," and has been slipping rightward toward early 2027.** The asymmetry is real but modest (≈1.9:1) and the wait is long with a soft clock. This is a high-quality WATCH/staging special-sit — credible plan, thin spread, no hard date — not a fire-now PENDING_HARD trigger.

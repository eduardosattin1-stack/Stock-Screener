# PRX.AS — Prosus NV (Delivery Hero divest) — Interrogator Dossier (Event-Driven Special Situation)

**Thesis class:** Forced-seller / overhang-clearing, idiosyncratic. The thesis is NOT Prosus-the-compounder (an ~80%-Tencent-NAV holdco at a ~35% NAV discount) — it is the discrete CLEARING of the Delivery Hero (DHER) overhang that the EU forced-divestiture commitment created, now colliding with a live Uber takeover of DHER that re-prices Prosus's stake from a distressed-seller liability into a strategically-sold asset.

## 1. THE EVENT — what actually backstops the spread

When the EU approved Prosus's €4.1bn acquisition of Just Eat Takeaway (Aug 2025), it forced a remedy: Prosus must cut its Delivery Hero stake to a "single-digit" percentage. That turned Prosus into a KNOWN forced seller of a ~16–17% DHER block on a regulatory clock — a textbook overhang that (a) pressed DHER's price and (b) sat as a discounted, illiquid, must-sell line item inside Prosus NAV. The Catalyst-Watch entry ("forced-seller recovery, Prosus clearing a Delivery Hero overhang") captures this.

What has happened in 2026 transforms the setup:

- **Apr 2026:** Prosus sold 4.5% of DHER **to Uber at €20.00/sh — a ~22% premium** to the 1-month VWAP (~€270m gross). A forced seller getting a 22% premium is the opposite of a fire sale.
- **May 2026:** Prosus sold ~5% **to Aspex Management at €22.00/sh** (~€335m). Premium again; a competitive bid book is forming around the block.
- **May 23, 2026:** Delivery Hero confirmed **Uber submitted an indicative proposal to acquire DHER at €33/share (~€10bn EV)**. Uber's direct + optioned interest reached ~25%.
- **Jun 2, 2026:** EU **granted a waiver EXTENDING the divestiture deadline to Oct 11, 2026**; Prosus shares jumped >10% on the news. Prosus (now ~16.8%) has even **asked the EU to DROP the forced-sale requirement entirely** so it can hold the stake into an Uber bid and negotiate from strength. Stifel carries a ~€40 probability-weighted DHER target.

**Net:** the load-bearing catalyst is real and LIVE — but its CHARACTER has flipped from "forced discount seller against a hard August clock" to "premium seller / potential bid-target shareholder with a relaxed Oct-11 clock and an option to keep the asset." The overhang is being CLEARED at premiums, not dumped at a discount.

## 2. CREDIBILITY OF THE SPONSOR / PROCESS

- **Acquirer credibility (Uber):** HIGH on intent — Uber has put real cash to work (bought the 4.5% block at a premium) and tabled a €33 indicative bid; this is a strategic consolidator, not a tyre-kicker. But the bid is INDICATIVE, not binding; no signed SPA, German takeover-law process (BaFin) and likely EU/antitrust review still ahead. So the DHER-bid leg is PENDING/soft, not locked.
- **Seller credibility (Prosus mgmt):** HIGH. They have demonstrably extracted premiums (Uber €20, Aspex €22), run a competitive process, and won a regulatory extension + are pressing to drop the remedy. The buyback (~10%/yr of float, ~$42bn returned through 9/2025 at a ~35% discount) is genuine NAV-accretive capital allocation that compounds the overhang-clearing.
- **Regulatory:** The EU extension to **Oct 11, 2026** is the hard date that matters for the FORCED leg. Prosus's request to drop the remedy is upside optionality, not a base case.

## 3. THE VERIFIED FLOOR — what bounds the loss

This is the crux for a special-sit. PRX is NOT a thin-floored single-binary — the DHER stake is a small fraction of NAV; the floor is the holdco itself:

- **Live price:** ~€38.2–39.5 (€38.22 on Jun 20; €39.09 prior close; dossier-card live €39.30).
- **Floor:** Prosus's NAV is ~80% Tencent (liquid, marked daily) plus net cash (>€13bn gross) and a self-funding ~10%/yr buyback at a ~35% discount. Even if the DHER catalyst delivered NOTHING incremental, the share is a mechanically-supported, deep-NAV-discount holdco. The card floor of **€37.37** (~5% below live) is conservative and credible — it is essentially "current NAV-discount holdco minus a modest DHER-disappointment haircut," NOT a deal-break cliff. The buyback is a live bid under the stock.
- **Target:** card target **€46.21** (~+18% to live, ~R:R 3.58 vs the €37.37 floor). This is a partial-discount-narrowing + overhang-removal re-rate, well short of the full ~35% NAV gap — i.e., conservative.

## 4. RED FLAGS

- 🔴 **Catalyst is DIFFUSE, not a single hard binary.** The "forced-seller recovery" is being realized in TRANCHES (Apr, May…) rather than one dated clearing event. The Oct-11 EU deadline is the only hard date, and Prosus is trying to make it irrelevant by asking to keep the stake. So the spread closes gradually, not on one re-rate day.
- 🔴 **DHER bid is INDICATIVE.** €33 Uber proposal is not binding; if it collapses, DHER's price (and thus the premium Prosus realizes on the residual stake) softens — though this is a small NAV line.
- 🔴 **Real driver of the PRX discount is TENCENT/China, not DHER.** Tencent Q1-2026 revenue +9% YoY (missed). The 35% discount is a China-overhang print; clearing DHER removes a minor irritant, not the dominant discount driver. Discount-narrowing is therefore not guaranteed by this catalyst alone.
- 🟢 **Mitigants:** premiums realized, regulatory clock relaxed, optionality to HOLD into a bid, and a self-funding buyback that mechanically narrows the discount regardless.

## 5. NO going-concern / dilution / covenant risk

Prosus is net-cash (>€13bn gross cash), no ATM/dilution dynamic, no covenant wall, no CVR games. This is the safest floor profile in the special-sit funnel — the downside risk is "modest underperformance of a deep-discount holdco," not capital impairment.

## 6. CAPITAL-ALLOCATION VERDICT

The EVENT is REAL and LIVE but **UNDATED as a single clearing trigger** and **SOFTENING** (extended to Oct 11 + a request to drop it entirely). The asymmetry is favorable (R:R ~3.58 to a verified, mechanically-supported floor) but the upside is a GRADUAL overhang-removal + buyback re-rate, not a hard-dated event-day pop. The single most important thing the director must weigh: this is a **PENDING-but-SOFT/serially-extended** catalyst with an EXCELLENT floor — it earns a solid watch-and-stage conviction (the floor is real, the R:R is genuine), but NOT the conviction-5 reserved for a hard-dated binding event inside a tight window. Moat/secular are N/A for the event (the underlying holdco's NAV is Tencent-driven; manageable, not terminal).

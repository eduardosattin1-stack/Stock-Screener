- **ECLIPSE 1** — FULLY ENROLLED. **Topline data Q4 2026** (the near-term hard catalyst). Combo vs. deferred treatment in US/limited-bulevirtide-access regions.
- **ECLIPSE 2 & 3** — data **Q1 2027** (2 & enrollment-ongoing; 3 fully enrolled).
- De-risking precedent: **SOLSTICE Ph2** showed 77% undetectable HDV RNA at Wk72, rising to **88% (21/24) at Wk96** on the combo vs 46% monotherapy — an unusually clean, replicated efficacy signal for a registrational bet. CHD has high unmet need and a thin competitive field (Gilead's bulevirtide not broadly US-available).
- **Second leg (optionality, not load-bearing):** oncology pivot funded by **Astellas ($335M upfront/near-term, 50/50 US split)**; VIR-5818 Ph1 response data 2H-2026; VIR-5500 at ASCO-GU Feb 2026. This de-dilutes and extends runway, but the equity re-rate hangs on ECLIPSE.

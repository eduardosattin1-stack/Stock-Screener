## 1. THE EVENT — is it REAL, DATED, and BINDING?

**REAL — yes.** DURAVYU (vorolanib intravitreal insert, formerly EYP-1901) is in two identical, double-masked, pivotal Phase 3 non-inferiority trials for wet age-related macular degeneration (wet AMD): **LUGANO** (~432 patients) and **LUCIA**. Primary endpoint = non-inferiority in change-from-baseline BCVA at weeks 52/56 (blended) vs on-label aflibercept (2 mg EYLEA) control. Secondary endpoints include superiority in treatment-burden reduction (DURAVYU dosed every 6 months vs anti-VEGF q8-week injections), supplement-free eyes, and anatomical outcomes. Both trials are FULLY ENROLLED (>900 patients combined). Trial design was agreed with FDA and EMA. (SEC 8-Ks; GlobeNewswire 2026-05-14; BioSpace.)

**DATED — yes, tight window, with a minor correction to the dossier date.** Company guidance as of the 2026-05-14 DSMC update: **"Data from the Phase 3 program are anticipated to be reported beginning in mid-2026,"** with management/analyst color narrowing LUGANO topline to **"beginning in August 2026,"** and LUCIA **~2 months later (H2 2026).** The Catalyst-Watch dossier's hard date of **2026-07-31** is therefore marginally EARLY vs the company's "beginning in August" framing — but it is the correct, imminent, dated window (~6-7 weeks out). This is a PENDING_HARD readout, not a soft/serially-slipping date: the timeline has held across multiple updates and is anchored to a fully-enrolled, completed 52/56-week endpoint.

**BINDING — yes.** This is a fixed-endpoint pivotal readout, not a third-party / financing-contingent event. The data event WILL occur; only the outcome is uncertain. The LUGANO readout is the single load-bearing milestone.
